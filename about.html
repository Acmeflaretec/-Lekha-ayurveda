About Ayurveda

Ayurveda is a traditional Cadian medical practice dating back to more than 3000 to 5000 years. It places great emphasis on prevention and encourages the maintanance of health through a close attention to balance in one's life, right thinking, diet, lifestyle and use of herbs. Knowledge of ayurveda lets you make lifestyle changes in a way that maintain this balance. It empowers your understanding that such a balance of body, mind and consiousness is essential for a healthy and productive lifestyle.
