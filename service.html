Panchakarma

It's a profound Ayurvedic detoxification and rejuvenation program that nourishes body, mind, and spirit. The term "Panchakarma" translates to "five actions" or "five treatments" in Sanskrit.

  • Vamana (therapeutic vomiting)
  • Virechana (purging)
  • Basti (medicated enema)
  • Nasya (nasal cleansing)
  • Shiravedanam

Weight loss management

We offers a comprehensive approach to weight loss management, focusing on aligning your life with what your body needs. Here are some Ayurvedic weight management practices

  • Understanding your Dosha: Recognizing your main flaws, whether vata, bile, or phlegm, is the key to successful weight loss, as each deficiency responds differently to diet, routine, and exercise.
  • Digestion: We help you in your diet and keep your digestive system working by eating at regular times, focusing on foods that are easy to digest and adding stimulating spices like ginger, cumin, and black pepper.
  • Detoxification and Cleansing: Detoxification and cleansing are necessary actions to improve overall health and initiate weight loss through a process called “panchakarma”.
  • Yoga and Exercise: Yoga and exercise are major pillars in Ayurvedic weight management, as they help improve digestion and reduce stress, making weight loss a natural process.

Stress management

We help you in Identifying your Dosha: Vata, Pitta, or Kapha, and understand its qualities and how it reacts to stress

Vitiated Vata (Vata Imbalance)
  • Balancing Vata qualities by increasing warmth and stillness
  • Meditative time each day
  • Healthy meals at regular times
  • Stress relief massage with warm oil
  • Following a healthy sleep routine
Vitiated Pitta (Pitta Imbalance)
  • Balancing Pitta with calm, coolness, and moderation
  • Meditation, Yoga, or Pranayama
  • Cool showers
  • Avoid spicy foods
  • Regular nature walks
Vitiated Kapha (Kapha Imbalance)
  • Staying active
  • Warming the body
  • Kapalabhati Pranayama (Shining Skull Breath) and other yoga asanas
  • Warming soups and vegetable stews
  • Avoid comfort foods
Treatments like Takradhara, shirodhara helps in the management of stress
