Our Treatments

PIZHICHIL

It is a mixture of oleation and sudation.Lukewarm herbal oils are applied all over the body by trained therapists in a special rhythmic way continously for about 60-90minutes/day for a period of 7-21 days. It is effective in arthritis, paralysis, rheumatic disease, and rejunivates the body completely

NJAVARA KIZHI

A form of massage used inayurveda to tone and strengthen the muscles and enhance nerve conductivity. A generous amount of medicated oil is applied all over the head and body. Then the body is massaged with warm muslin bag boluses, containing njavara rice pudding cooked in a herbal decoction. The boluses are dipped in the mixture of warm milk or herbal decoction. It is useful in various muscular, neurological and vertebral diseases.

YOGA

Yoga is an art form which helps in attaining good health and for living a holistic life. Yoga deals with the purification of mind and consciousness

ELAKIZHI

Rare and valued herbs cooked in a medicated oil and packed in cotton bags are applied all over the body along with gentle massage. This treatment lubricates the joints, relieves body pain and reopens the channel of circulation.

SHIRODHARA

In this process, some herbal oils and/or buttermilk is poured on the forehead in a specific method. This treatment is mainly for sleeplessness, migraine, relieving mental tension and certain skin diseases.

Abhyagam (Relaxation massage)

In this massage medicated oil is applied all over the body to increase blood circulation and relax the mind. It also helps in delaying aging, improves vitality and sleep.

Nasyam

Medicated oil or juice is applied through nose for 7 to 40 days. This treatment is highly effective in headache, paralysis, mental disorders and skin diseases.

Tharpanam

In ayurveda, tharpanam or tharpan means retention of medicines over the eyes for up to 30 minutes or more daily as a treatment for problems related to the eye.

Post Natal Care

By ayurvedic treatment we can strengthen the muscle and ligaments, and ensure good quality and quantity of milk. We provide care for both the mother and the newborn baby.

Udwarthanam

Udwarthanam is a popular ayurvedic deep tissue massage and ayurvedic weight loss therapy using special herbal powders. It is a refreshing and rejuvenating massage for the whole body. Weight loss and cellulite reduction loss are the main aims of this treatment.

Foot Therapy

is a powerful full body massage which has an impact on all tissues within the body. The therapist performs the massage with their feet, reaching the deepest layers of the body. The session concludes with a hand massage of the head and face. The treatment is based on the Marma and Nadi System (system of energy points and pathways). It benefits the flexibility of the body, relaxes the muscles, removes body waste and blockages, supplies the cells with Prana (life energy) and balances the Doshas.

Raktamoksha (Therapeutic blood letting)

A form of therapeutic blood letting, it means removing impure blood from the body. It is a localized treatment, where not more than 10-40ml blood is drawn at a time. It is helpful for skin disease, herpes, jaundice, ulcer, gout, piles, abscess, leucoderma, eczema, deep venous thrombosis, and similar other diseases.

Agnikarma

Also known as the dahana karma, it is a process used in ayurveda for various benign disease that are characterized by pain or bleeding. This treatment is mainly used in skin tags, tennis elbow, calcaneus spur, corns, warts, and alopecia etc.

Skin and hair care

We provide beauty care treatments for unisex which includes facial, packs, hair masks and herbal formulations. Some of these remedies greatly help in the treatment of skin issues like acne, eczema, dryness, redness, dandruff, and hairloss etc.

Marma chikitsa

Marma chikitsa is one among the most powerful treatments in ayurveda providing an overall healing experience. Marma therapy directs the mind energy to the diseased part by strengthening the bridge between the mind's and the body.
